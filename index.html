Amir Mashmool is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Bremen, Germany , under the supervision of Professor Rainer Koschke. He received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Birjand, Iran , where he studied from 2016 to 2021 and was nominated for the Talented Student Award in May 2021. Following this, he completed his M.Sc. in Computer Science at the Department of Informatics, Bioengineering, Robotics, and System Engineering (DIBRIS) at the University of Genova, Italy , from 2021 to 2024.
His research interests lie in software engineering, empirical software engineering,human aspects of SE,mining software repositories, cloud/edge computing, and machine learning
