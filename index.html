Amir Mashmool

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Computer Science at the University of Bremen, Germany , working under the supervision of Professor Rainer Koschke.

Previously, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the University of Birjand, Iran , and my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Genova, Italy .

My research focuses on:

  • Software Visualization
  • Empirical Software Engineering
  • Human Aspects of Software Engineering
  • Cloud / Edge Computing
  • Machine Learning
