Amir Mashmool is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Bremen, Germany , under the supervision of Professor Rainer Koschke.
He received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Birjand, Iran , and M.Sc. in Computer Science at University of Genova, Italy .
His research interests include software engineering, empirical software engineering, human aspects of SE, mining software repositories, cloud/edge computing, and machine learning.
If you are a researcher working on empirical software engineering and the human aspect of software engineering and are interested in my work, feel free to reach out to propose/discuss possible collaborations. I'm always open to collaborating on software engineering. Let's create some joint publications together. 📝
