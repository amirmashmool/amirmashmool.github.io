    Have you ever heard of the Erdos number?
    It's a fun way to measure the collaboration and connection between mathematicians. The idea is simple: if you've written a paper with someone who has an Erdos number of 1, then your Erdos number is 2. If you've written a paper with someone who has an Erdos number of 2, your Erdos number is 3, and so on.
