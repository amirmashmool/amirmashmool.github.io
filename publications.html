Bio Summary (for papers/keynote)

AMIR MASHMOOL received his B.Sc. degree in computer engineering from the University of Birjand, Iran. He is currently an M.Sc. student in computer science at the Department of Informatics, Bioengineering, Robotics, and System Engineering (DIBRIS) at the University of Genova, Italy. He has worked as a research assistant under Dr. Mohammad GhasemiGol’s and Dr. Hamid Saadatfar’s supervision at the Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Birjand. He was nominated for a Talented Student Award by the University of Birjand in May 2021. He has published several articles in prestigious conferences and journals (Journal of Systems and Software, IEEE Access). He has served as a peer reviewer for the IEEE Access journal. His research focuses on software engineering, empirical software engineering, machine learning, and deep learning.
